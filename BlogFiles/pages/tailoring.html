23rd Feb

I wanted to be a tailor since I watched the Kingsman films (in particular "The King's Man"), and I actually went and kinda actually did it. I am in the process of starting my own company, called "R. L. Thomas & Co. Gentleman's Outfitters". I have a massive passion for making things, and having vintage things, so making vintage style clothes the traditional ways is absolutely grand for me. SoonTM I will have my own industrial sewing machine, hopefully vintage and entirely mechanical (cause I love using and maintaining purely mechanical systems), and then I will be able to start taking on bigger and better projects than what my current domestic machine can handle. Will update this when that happens cause excite!
